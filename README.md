# Privacy Policy

**Rommie: Student Planning** respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit personal data.

## Information Collection and Use

Rommie does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information.

All data you enter into the app — including GPA information, schedules, countdowns, and preferences — is stored **locally on your device** using system storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing

Rommie does not share data with:
- Third parties
- Advertisers
- Analytics providers
- External servers

## Internet Access

Rommie does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit data over the network.

## Data Deletion

You may delete all app data at any time by using the **“Clear All Data”** option within the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.

## Children’s Privacy

Rommie does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Security

Because all data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally, your information is protected by your device’s built-in security mechanisms.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of this document.
